Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is GYD free?

A: Yes, GYD is 100% free for all users at the moment. If you would like to help GYD, we are open to donations :)

Q: Do you sell any of my data?

A: No, here at GYD we prioritize our user's privacy and security over anything else. GYD is firebase hosted with a SSL certificate. All data is encrypted to and from the server. Additionally, we do not use your data to train our machine learning models. We never save your password and you can delete all your history in a click of a button.

Q: How do we contact you?

A: All the creator's emails are found under the About Us section in the Home page.
