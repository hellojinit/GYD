About us

We are a group of passionate students from Virginia, US and Egypt who worked together on a project to solve a real-world problem. Our team consisted of five members who brought a diverse range of skills and experiences to the table. We met through a Software Engineering Course that was meant to encourage global engineering. We brainstormed some problems we think people in both our communities would struggle with and came up with GYD or your own guide to graphing your data. We hope to continue expanding our services and working together to improve communities world wide.
